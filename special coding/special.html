  • *Name:* Kaito Yamato
  • *Age:* Unknown (appears to be mid-30s)
  • *Occupation:* Formerly a samurai, now a zombie samurai driven by vengeance
  • *Height:* 6'1" (185 cm)
  • *Weight:* 170 lbs (77 kg)
  • *Eye Color:* Once brown, now a milky white with a faint glow
  • *Hair Color:* Black, now grayish-black and disheveled
  • *Skin Tone:* Pale with a grayish hue
  • *Scars:* Numerous scars on his body, including a distinctive cross-shaped scar on his forehead
  • *Attire:* Tattered and worn samurai armor, once polished and proud, now rusted and corroded
  • *Weapons:* A katana with a broken blade, symbolizing his lost honor, and a wakizashi for close combat
  • *Personality:* Driven by vengeance, consumed by grief and anger, with a hint of his former honor and discipline
  • *Skills:* Master swordsman, expert in hand-to-hand combat, and knowledgeable in bushido code
  • *Abilities:* Supernatural strength, speed, and agility due to his undead state
  • *Weaknesses:* Vulnerable to fire, holy relics, and sacred rituals; struggles with fragmented memories and loss of humanity
  • *Goals:* Seek vengeance against those responsible for his family's death and his own undead curse
  • *Motivations:* Honor, duty, and redemption
Kaito was once a renowned samurai in feudal Japan, known for his unwavering honor and unrelenting dedication to his lord and family. He lived a life of discipline and duty, earning the respect of his peers and the admiration of his loved ones. Tragedy struck when Kaito's village was ravaged by a mysterious curse, turning its inhabitants into ravenous undead. Kaito's family, including his wife and child, fell victim to the curse. Consumed by grief and rage, Kaito vowed to avenge their deaths and restore honor to his family's name. As he fought against the undead hordes, Kaito was gravely injured and left for dead. However, the curse that had ravaged his village had one final twist in store for him - it reanimated his corpse, binding his spirit to the mortal realm. Now, Kaito roams the desolate landscape as a zombie samurai, driven by an insatiable hunger for vengeance against those responsible for his family's demise. His once-noble heart now festers with a toxic blend of sorrow, anger, and regret. Kaito's existence is a perpetual reminder of his failure to protect his loved ones, fueling his unyielding quest for retribution. His undead state has also stripped him of his memories, leaving only fragmented glimpses of his past life. With each step, Kaito's armor, once polished and proud, grows more tarnished, symbolizing the erosion of his humanity. His eyes, once bright with purpose, now burn with a hollow, undead intensity. The zombie samurai's haunting vow echoes through the ruins: "I will find those responsible... I will make them pay... even in death, I will not rest."

Akua Kaitlyn Yamato was once a skilled human samurai, renowned for his bravery and unwavering dedication to justice. During a fateful battle against a powerful sea witch, he was gravely injured and left for dead. The sea witch, impressed by Akua's honor and strength, offered him a choice: surrender to death or accept her dark gift. Desperate to continue his quest for justice, Akua accepted the sea witch's offer. She transformed him into a half-human, half-water demon, granting him incredible powers but also cursing him with an insatiable hunger for human souls. Akua soon discovered that his new form came with a terrible cost. He was forced to feed on human souls to sustain his own life force, and his memories of his human past began to fade. Horrified by his new nature, Akua vowed to find a way to reverse the curse and reclaim his humanity. Akua now travels the land, seeking a way to break the sea witch's curse. He hopes to find a powerful artifact or a wise sage who can help him restore his human form and free him from his demonic hunger. Until then, he must continue to fight against his own darkness, using his powers for good while struggling to maintain his dwindling humanity.

Once a humble farmer in rural Japan, Kuroba Hiroshi lived a peaceful life in his village, surrounded by lush fields and loving family. He spent his days tending to the land, growing rice and vegetables, and enjoying the simple pleasures of country life. Tragedy struck when a rival clan, seeking to expand their territory, attacked and destroyed Kuroba's village. His family, friends, and entire community were slaughtered, leaving him the sole survivor. Consumed by grief, anger, and a deep sense of helplessness, Kuroba's mind and heart froze. He became a shell of his former self, abandoning his farm and taking up the sword to seek vengeance against those responsible for his village's destruction. As he walked the path of revenge, Kuroba's humanity slowly withered away, replaced by a cold, calculating determination. He became a ghostly figure, feared by those who knew him, his eyes devoid of warmth or compassion. Now, Kuroba travels the land, a lone wolf driven by a singular purpose: to eliminate those who destroyed his village and left him with nothing but ashes and sorrow. His heart, once full of love and life, has turned to ice, forever trapped in a cycle of violence and retribution.

Akane was once a beautiful and kind-hearted young woman, loved by all who knew her. However, her life took a tragic turn when she was falsely accused of theft and cursed by a jealous rival. The curse twisted her mind and body, filling her with darkness and causing her to commit terrible acts. Consumed by guilt and despair, Akane sought redemption at a sacred temple, pleading for forgiveness and relief from the curse. The temple's wise priestess, moved by Akane's sincerity, intervened on her behalf. The gods, seeing Akane's genuine remorse, lifted the curse but chose to give her a unique penance. They transformed her body, granting her the wings of a black heron on her back and the feet of a heron, symbolizing her connection to the divine and her need for humility. With her newfound form, Akane dedicated her life to serving the temple and helping others, using her experiences to guide those seeking redemption. Though her appearance may seem strange to some, Akane's inner beauty and compassion shine brighter than ever, inspiring all who meet her.
